Discussion

Open question 1 · opinion

Do you think personality is fixed, or do you think habits can change who we are? Why?

Open question 2 · personal experience

Have you ever changed one small habit and noticed a change in how you felt about yourself? Tell us about it.

Open question 3 · comparison

How are chosen habits, like Maya’s morning walk, different from automatic habits, like opening Instagram when bored?

Open question 4 · opinion

Why do you think Maya felt different after saying hello first, even though she still felt nervous?

Open question 5 · personal experience

What are some habits in your daily life that may be shaping your identity right now, in good or bad ways?
