Key Concepts

PGI

A protected name that links a wine to a specific place.

It helps a buyer connect the bottle to climate, region, and likely style.

acidity

The fresh, sharp edge that makes a wine feel lively.

Higher acidity often makes white and sparkling wines feel cleaner and brighter.

tannin

The dry grip in wine that comes from grape skins and seeds.

It gives red wine structure and helps it match richer food.

bottle ageing

Time spent resting in bottle so texture and flavor change slowly.

Stable ageing can make sparkling wine feel calmer and more connected.

indigenous grape

A grape variety strongly linked to a local place and tradition.

It helps explain why local wines do not all taste like international styles.

cellar climate

The temperature and humidity conditions inside a wine cellar.

A stable cellar climate protects wine while it matures.

Codru: White and Sparkling Focus

Codru -> cooler central conditions -> slower ripening -> higher acidity -> fresher white and sparkling wines

Cooler central conditions slow grape ripening, so the fruit keeps more freshness.

Underground Cellar Climate

Underground limestone galleries -> stable temperature and humidity -> slower bottle ageing -> cleaner sparkling wine texture

Stable underground galleries support gradual ageing, which can improve sparkling texture.

Stefan Voda Red Structure

Stefan Voda -> warmer southeast conditions -> fuller ripening -> deeper color and firmer tannin in red wines

Warmer southeast conditions help red grapes ripen fully, so color and tannin increase.

Valul lui Traian Style Range

Valul lui Traian -> sunnier southwest conditions -> riper fruit -> more intense reds and lively rose styles

More sun pushes fruit ripeness, which supports both lively rose and deeper red styles.

Feteasca Neagra Style Signal

Feteasca Neagra -> darker fruit and tannin -> fuller red wine style

This local grape often signals a fuller red with more grip and darker fruit notes.

Analysis:Codru is the safer choice for fish because acidity keeps the wine fresh. Stefan Voda suits roast meat because warmer ripening builds color and tannin. Valul lui Traian rose is the easiest match for a lighter meal.

Takeaway:In Moldova, region and cellar climate help a buyer predict style before opening the bottle.

Mini Case Study

Scenario: A diner in Chișinău must choose one Moldovan bottle for fish, roast meat, and a lighter summer meal.

Analysis: Codru is the safer choice for fish because acidity keeps the wine fresh. Stefan Voda suits roast meat because warmer ripening builds color and tannin. Valul lui Traian rose is the easiest match for a lighter meal.

Takeaway: In Moldova, region and cellar climate help a buyer predict style before opening the bottle.

7-Day Tracking Checklist

  1. Day 1: Map the three main PGI regions - Say one style clue for Codru, Stefan Voda, and Valul lui Traian.
  2. Day 2: Review acidity - Explain why higher acidity makes wine feel fresher.
  3. Day 3: Review tannin - Connect tannin to structure and food pairing.
  4. Day 4: Review cellar climate - Explain why stable temperature and humidity matter.
  5. Day 5: Review bottle ageing - Describe what slower bottle ageing can do to sparkling texture.
  6. Day 6: Review indigenous grapes - Compare Feteasca Neagra, Rara Neagra, and Viorica in simple words.
  7. Day 7: Do one dinner recommendation - Choose a Moldovan bottle and justify it with region, grape, and food.

Technical Practice

Comprehension

  • What does PGI mean?
  • Why do Codru whites and sparkling wines often taste fresher?
  • How do limestone cellars help sparkling wine age?
  • Why do Stefan Voda red wines often feel stronger?
  • Which styles fit fish, roast meat, and a lighter summer meal?

Discussion

  • Why is PGI useful when you choose a bottle of wine?
  • Which matters more for style, region or grape variety? Why?
  • How do underground cellars add value to Moldovan wine?
  • Which Moldovan style would you choose for your own dinner, and why?

Speaking

  • Explain the Codru chain in 2 or 3 sentences: cooler region, slower ripening, higher acidity, fresher wine.
  • Compare Stefan Voda and Valul lui Traian in 2 or 3 clear sentences.
  • Recommend one Moldovan bottle for fish, roast meat, or a lighter summer meal.
