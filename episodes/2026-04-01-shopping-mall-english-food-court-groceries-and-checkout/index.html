Wrap-Up

Discussion

Open question 1 · opinion

Do you think Mia and Dan made good choices by eating lunch first and then shopping? Why or why not?

Open question 2 · personal experience

Have you ever gone shopping when you had very little time like Mia and Dan? What happened?

Open question 3 · comparison

How is shopping in a busy mall different from shopping in a small store or supermarket?

Open question 4 · opinion

What do you think is the most useful shopping word from this lesson: food court, on sale, total, or receipt? Why?
